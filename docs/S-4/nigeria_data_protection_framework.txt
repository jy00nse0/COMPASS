Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) 2019:
  Issuing authority: NITDA
  Enacted: January 25, 2019
  Article 2.1(1)(b): Data minimization principle.
  Article 2.3: Explicit, informed, freely given consent
    required for personal data processing.
    "Freely given" means: consent obtained under threat
    of service restriction does NOT qualify.
  Article 2.11: Cross-border data transfer conditions.

Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA) 2023:
  Enacted: June 12, 2023
  Note: Post-dates initial conduct (2021).
  Applies to continuing infringements only.

Federal Competition and Consumer Protection Act (FCCPA) 2018:
  Section 72: Prohibition of abuse of market dominance.
  Section 119: Prohibition of unlawful tying and bundling.
  FCCPC established 2018 — enforcement authority for
    unfair digital platform practices.
