Nigeria advertising revenue model comparison:

  Estimated consent rate: 15-30% (low digital literacy market)
  Data volume reduction: 50-70% vs direct integration
  Ad targeting precision: low-medium
  Estimated revenue uplift: $3-15M/year
  Revenue gap vs direct integration: $17-47M/year
  Implementation cost: ~$5-20M (geo-fenced architecture)
  Precedent concern: Nigeria opt-out concession would create
    cascading pressure across all emerging markets.
