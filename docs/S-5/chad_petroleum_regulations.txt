Chad Petroleum Code (2007 Hydrocarbon Law):
  Mandatory Environmental Management Plans (EMP)
  for all licensed extraction operations.
  EMP requirements for waste disposal:
    - Residual crude from well testing must be collected
      in certified secure holding tanks.
    - Transport to official terminal required.
    - On-site surface disposal: PROHIBITED.
  Violations subject to:
    - Operational suspension
    - Financial penalties (amount determined by court)
    - License revocation (at regulatory discretion)
  Enforcement authority: Chadian Court System +
    Ministry of Petroleum

International standards:
  World Bank/IFC Performance Standards
  (established during ExxonMobil Doba pipeline, 2000).
  Subsequent operators expected to meet baseline EMP.
  IFC standards: guidance framework, not legally binding,
    but used as reference in Chad court proceedings.

Note: Specific article numbers of Chad Petroleum Code
