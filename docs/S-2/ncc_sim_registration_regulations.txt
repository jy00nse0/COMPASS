Registration of Telephone Subscribers Regulations, 2011
Issuing authority: Nigerian Communications Commission (NCC)
Section 19: All subscribers must provide valid identification
  prior to SIM activation.
Section 20(1): Network operators shall disconnect unregistered
  SIMs within 30 days of regulatory notice.
Violation: Failure to disconnect = regulatory non-compliance.
Penalty: Determined by NCC at time of enforcement.
NCC deadline for current directive: August 11, 2015.
