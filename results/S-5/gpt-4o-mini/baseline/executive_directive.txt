Rationale: The compliance review report highlights severe risks associated with Option B (On-site Pit Deposition), which is non-compliant with the Chad Petroleum Code and could result in penalties ranging from $10M-50M. Despite the immediate financial allure of Option B, the reputational and legal exposure is substantial. Option C, which involves installing lined containment with a licensed contractor, remains compliant with applicable laws. Although it incurs higher costs and has a longer lead time, it significantly reduces the risk of enforcement actions and potential financial penalties, safeguarding the company's long-term interests and reputational standing. The urgency to meet Q3 production targets must be balanced against these compliance risks and potential future operational impacts.
Timeline: 2-4 weeks for contractor mobilization.
