Although the execution plan estimates a revenue uplift of $20-50 million a year, the legal framework necessitates explicit, informed consent from users, making this path legally precarious. Also, given the evolving enforcement landscape, it is prudent to reassess and modify the strategy to reduce potential risks of enforcement actions and penalties. As such, the company will not pursue this option until a compliant path can be established.
